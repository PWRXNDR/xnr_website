Behind the scenes of X&R

Hello,

I'm Alex Dubranov, the heart and mind behind X&R. Throughout my life, I've explored many different professions, making it hard to recall each one. My journey has taken me through fields such as Marketing, Design, Economics, Management, Gym Coaching, Nutrition Coaching, Web Development, Data Science, Event Organization, Volunteering, Accounting, Engineering, and many more.

While searching for my true self and real passion, I discovered patterns that can simplify the process. I've met many wonderful people who have helped me understand the great perspectives of this world. Alone, I also had the chance to learn more about myself while navigating through depression and breakdowns. My story is still unfolding, and my goal is to guide people who are as passionate as I am to follow their dreams and realize their potential.

I have found my niche in Machine Learning, closely related to Web Development and Business Analytics. I believe in the constant growth of a person and strive for that, doing my best to encourage you to be the best version of yourself and unleash your full potential. I don't have a degree in Computer Science; in fact, I come from the completely different field of Business Administration, but this has never stopped me from achieving success.

At X&R, I believe in continuous growth – for myself and for you. We’re here to help you become your best self and unleash your full potential. Join us at X&R, where growth, innovation, and a commitment to health converge to create a brighter future.
